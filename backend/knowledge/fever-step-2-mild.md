# Fever Protocol — Step 2 (Mild)

Mild to moderate fever without red flags can often be managed at home with rest, hydration, and over-the-counter fever reducers. However, if the fever persists beyond 3 days or new symptoms develop, the patient should seek medical evaluation.

In malaria-endemic regions, persistent fever of any severity should prompt a malaria test.
