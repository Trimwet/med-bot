# Chest Pain Protocol — Step 2 (Radiating)

Radiating chest pain — especially to the left arm, jaw, or back — is a classic cardiac event pattern. Escalate conservatively. Even moderate radiating pain combined with nausea or lightheadedness warrants emergency assessment.

Advise the patient to go to the nearest emergency department if any moderate-to-severe symptoms are present.
