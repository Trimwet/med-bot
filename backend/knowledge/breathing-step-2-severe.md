# Breathing Difficulty Protocol — Step 2 (Severe)

Severe breathing difficulty requires immediate emergency attention. If the patient has blue lips or fingers, is confused, or cannot speak in full sentences, advise them to call 112 or go to the nearest emergency department right now.

Do not suggest home remedies or waiting. This is a potential life-threatening situation.
