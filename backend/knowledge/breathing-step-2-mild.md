# Breathing Difficulty Protocol — Step 2 (Mild)

Mild to moderate breathing difficulty without red flags may be managed with rest and monitoring. If the patient has asthma, remind them to use their reliever inhaler as prescribed.

Advise the patient to seek medical attention if symptoms worsen, do not improve within a few hours, or if they develop chest pain or fever.
