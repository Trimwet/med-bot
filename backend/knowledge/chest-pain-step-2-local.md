# Chest Pain Protocol — Step 2 (Localised)

Localised chest pain that is reproducible with movement or pressure is often musculoskeletal in origin. However, if the pain is severe (7+) or accompanied by shortness of breath, escalate.

For mild-to-moderate localised pain without red flags, rest and over-the-counter pain relief may be appropriate.
